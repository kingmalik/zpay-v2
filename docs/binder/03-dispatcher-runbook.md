## Coverage addendum (added 2026-07-23, screen: `/dispatch/assign`)
- **Callout or ghost → Coverage tab first, phone second.** Pick the route + date; the tool shows free drivers (green cards) and, when nobody's free, ready-made swap chains (yellow cards, never more than two moves, nobody displaced twice). Start calls from the top card.
- **Every recurring route keeps 1–2 standing backups** (Rosters tab). A route with a yellow dot has no backup — fix those in quiet moments, not during fires. "Refresh from history" re-derives the roster list after season changes.
- **New ride email from Brandon → New Ride tab.** Paste the whole email; check the parsed card; read the margin banner (red banner = the ride loses money at the usual rate — that's a pass unless Malik says otherwise); pick from the ranked drivers; Take or Pass with the one-tap reason; copy the drafted reply into the email thread.
- **When the strip at the top asks where a driver lives, answer it** — one question, rough area is fine. Each answer makes every future suggestion smarter.
