# Hiring Runbook — From "I know a guy" to First Ride

> Franchise Binder §4 · drafted 2026-07-22 · reader: the operator onboarding a new driver. The system tracks every step per hire in Z-Pay; this page is the map and the phone numbers.

## The rule before the steps
A driver's blocker is almost never ability — it's language. The trilingual walkthroughs (English/Amharic/Arabic, written + audio) exist so the hire does each step **on their own phone, themselves**. Send the link, let them work. You step in only when a step stalls — the fallback cockpit in Z-Pay shows exactly where each hire is stuck.

## The 10 steps, in order
1. **FirstAlt invite** — request from Brandon (Branden.Seeberger@firststudentinc.com). Hire gets an FA account email.
2. **Background check** — through FA's flow; hire completes it self-serve with the walkthrough.
3. **Drug test consent** — e-sign in Z-Pay (system sends it; timestamps land on the driver record).
4. **Drug test** — schedule with Donna at Concentra (she runs it end-to-end). Priority Solutions backup: admin@prioritysolutions.org / 425-255-0575.
5. **FirstAlt training** — FA's own platform, hire self-serves with the walkthrough. Nobody takes a training *for* a hire — ever. That era is over.
6. **Documents** — license, registration, insurance, vehicle photos uploaded in Z-Pay. Every document with an expiry date gets that date entered — this feeds the compliance tracker that nags before anything lapses.
7. **Partner contract** — Acumen (FA drivers) or Maz (ED drivers), typed-name e-sign in Z-Pay.
8. **Maz training** — our trilingual mini-course in Z-Pay (see §5, Driver Rules & Certification).
9. **Maz contract** — typed-name e-sign in Z-Pay.
10. **Paychex + W-9** — W-9 collected, driver entered in Paychex (CSV export from Z-Pay, manual import). **Tenant check:** FA drivers → Acumen International, ED drivers → Maz Services.

## Gates that are not optional
- **No first ride until step 8's quiz is passed and step 9 is signed.** The system enforces it; don't route around it.
- Annual renewals (drug test, background check, training, registration, mechanic certification) are tracked by the same expiry system — treat a renewal nag like a step 6 upload, same week it fires.

## What to watch per hire
The Z-Pay onboarding view shows each hire's current step, how long they've sat on it, and whether they did it self-serve or needed help. **A hire stuck >3 days on any step gets one call** — usually it's a comprehension knot the walkthrough audio solves. The % of steps done self-serve is on the owner dashboard: that number *is* how scalable hiring is.
